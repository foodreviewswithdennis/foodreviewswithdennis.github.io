Why I liked Casa Robles

January 16th 2026

Casa Robles is a Andalusian restaurant in Seville, Spain that I LOVED and wanted to shout out! The ambience in Casa Robles is amazing, just off the main cathedral square in a small alleyway. Also, the food is world class. I loved everything in the Starter section like the “Homemade croquettes of the house”, the “Iberian acorn ham” and the “Assortment of the best andalucian cheeses with jam”. In Andalusian food, freshness, quality and tradition is something that really shows while eating at Casa Robles.

An Easy Recipe for Scrumptious Steak Frites

December 22nd 2025

Steak Frites is also one of my favorite foods. The appeal of steak frites is that nice steak with those crispy frites.

Ingredients:

1. Potatoes - as little or as many as you like.
2. Oil for frying
3. A tasty steak – at room temperature
4. Olive Oil
5. Salt and Black Pepper

Directions:

1. Peel the potatoes and slice them lengthwise into ⅓ inch thick frites.
2. Add oil to the pot over medium-high heat.
3. Once the oil reaches 300°F, carefully add the potatoes to the hot oil and cook until beginning to soften (about 3-4 minutes).
4. Then, transfer the potatoes to a paper towel lined pan.
5. Heat a large skillet over high heat.
6. Pat the steak dry and season all over with the salt.
7. Add the olive oil to the skillet.
8. Once the oil is just smoking, add the steak and cook for about 6 to 8 minutes per side.
9. Then, transfer the steak to a cutting board and top with the butter and let the steak rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
10. Increase the oil temperature to 350°F.
11. Return the potatoes to the hot oil and cook until golden brown and crispy, about 5 minutes.
12. Transfer the fries to the sheet pan.
13. Season with salt and pepper to taste and enjoy.


The History of Bolognese

December 12th 2025

Bolognese is one of my favorite foods and I wanted to tell you about it. The exact origin is unclear but Bolognese was made in Bologna, Italy. During the Italian Renaissance, Bolognese became a true staple of Italian cuisine and it was enjoyed all over Italy. Bolognese is often served on tagliatelle pasta. Finally, after a long-time bolognese was enjoyed all over the world, from America to Argentina, you can find bolognese in many restaurants. Fun Fact: The Italian Academy of Cooking has made an official recipe for bolognese.

The History of Steak Frites

December 7th 2025

Steak frites is one of my favorite foods and I wanted to learn about its history, and I never knew about its long history in France and Belgium. It all goes back to the Middle Ages where the French would serve steak with boiled and roasted potatoes. While the French were perfecting steak, the Belgians were busy perfecting the art of frying potatoes. The concept of steak and frites emerged in the 19th century and rose in popularity in France. And then good French steak paired with crispy Belgian frites became very popular. When it became popular, then regional variations were made to improve on the traditional steak frites. Finally, steak frites became a dish popular across the whole world.
